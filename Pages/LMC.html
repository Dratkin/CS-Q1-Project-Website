Lost Media Creepypastas

Lost Media creepypastas are creepypastas that involve forms of entertainment like TV shows and sometimes, even films. Most creepypastas of this genre tell fictional stories about pre-existing shows and films however, there are occasionally creepypastas that tell stories about enitrely fictional shows and films (and, in my opinion, these types of lost media creepypastas are the best ones).
