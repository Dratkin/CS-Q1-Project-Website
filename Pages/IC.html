Internet Creepypastas

Internet Creepypastas are Creepypastas that involve certain elements or websites that can be found on the internet. Most of these Creepypastas take place in various places on the internet like social media websites, online forums, and even the dark web. Internet Creepypastas are special because of how creative they can be at times which may come from the broad range of topics that Creepypastas of this genre can tackle.
