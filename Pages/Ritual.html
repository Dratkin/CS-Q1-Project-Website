Ritualpastas

Ritualpastas are creepypastas that give information about a supposed ritual and instructions on how to preform the ritual. Most Ritualpastas are based off of folk lore, practices, and beliefs from culture-rich places like Japan however, there are still a lot of Ritualpastas that create entirely new rituals. Ritualpastas can generally be organized into 2 categories: Ritualpastas where the ritual presents a reward for the participant/s of the ritual and Ritualpastas where the ritual generally presents no reward and is only done for thrill-seeking.
